Speakers

KeyNote Speaker

Dolapo Oni-Sijuwade (Actress/Media personality)

Marcy Dolapo Oni is an actress and media personality who works on TV across the entire continent of Africa interviewing high profile guests engaging them daily with her wit, charm and charisma.

Marcy has interviewed some of the biggest celebrities worldwide from musicians, actors and sportsmen to business moguls, such as Sean Paul, Kelly Rowland, Femi Kuti, Venus and Serena Williams to name a few.

Marcy gained her primary education at the Lebanese community school in Lagos, Nigeria. She was then relocated by her parents to England, where she gained her secondary education at Headington Independent Girls ’School, Oxford, where she was made the first black head girl.

With a bachelor’s degree in chemistry from the University of Bristol, she then acquired a postgraduate degree In Drama from ALRA (Academy of Live and Recorded Arts) in London.

Marcy began her African television career in 2010, after a very successful acting career in the U.K as the lead on the Mnet show '53 Extra'. From there she was nominated amongst hundreds of contestants as one of the two chosen co-hosts on Africa’s number one talk show “Moments with Mo” which is exclusive to EbonyLife TV: Africa’s premium entertainment channel.

She has since worked globally from stage and television, to radio crafting her expertise in entertainment. As one of Nigeria’s No 1 female TV personalities, Marcy has hosted many high profile corporate and lifestyle events for companies such as Nokia, P&G, Africa Magic, Malta Guinness, Ebony Life TV Launch (with Dare Art Alade), Genevieve Magazine, Complete Fashion Magazine, Access Bank, Sujimoto Construction and The Future Awards. She was chosen to represent Nigeria as a VIP guest on behalf of Moët and Chandon Champagne at the 2012 Roland Garros Tournament in France.

Panelist

Temitope Ooye (Digital Marketing Consultant)

Temitope Ooye (@ThatMediaBabe) is a Communication and Marketing Professional passionate about building, managing and connecting brands with the right audience and delivering on set objectives.

Having worked both on the client and agency side, she has over the past years gathered experience across Brand Strategy and Communications, Client Servicing, Digital Marketing, Account/Project management, Media Planning/Buying/Production.

She holds a Professional Diploma in Digital Marketing from the Prestigious Digital Marketing Institute (DMI), Dublin, Ireland and another Diploma in Online Reputation Management (ORM) from Shaw Academy. A graduate of Chemistry (B.Sc.) from Olabisi Onabanjo University and member of the Institute of Strategic Management, Nigeria (ISMN).

Panelist

Chika Ilang (Personal and Process Improvement Expert)

Chika Ilang is the lead principal at Chika Ilang International, and the creator of the Focus Design Method; which put simply, is the art and science of creating bigger and better outcome in life and business.

She works with individuals and organizations to improve their processes towards achieving exponential growth and increased productivity.

Chika uses her “impossibility is nothing” approach to stretch and spur her clients to reach their maximum potential. Through her personalized service, she has helped over 100 individuals move from fuzzy to clarity, where they’re certain of the exact steps to take to create the exact outcome they desire in their lives.

Panelist

Nkem Okocha (Social Entrepreneur/Founder, Mamamoni)

Nkem Okocha is a serial award-winning social entrepreneur. She is passionate about the change that is possible from investing in poor women in underserved communities. And her vision is guided by the saying that "No woman chooses to be poor, but we can choose to help poor women by investing in them".

Nkem is the founder of Mamamoni, a FinTech social enterprise that empowers low-income rural-and-urban slum women with free vocational and financial skills, and small loans.

Mamamoni's web platform www.mamamoni.org allows socially-conscious individuals to lend to low-income women money for their small businesses. In the last 2 years, Mamamoni has been able to set-up a liquid wash enterprise, Levantar, which is fast becoming a household name.

Panelist

Jokotade Shonowo (Creative Director, PoshClick Portraiture)

Jokotade Shonowo is the Founder and Lead Photographer at Poshclick Portraiture; a leading boutique photography company that specializes in creating timeless images with character and soul for the accomplished individual.

She is vested in giving back to humanity, has the interest of the girl child and generally the young-ins at heart and as such over the years she’s partnered with Ebi School Leavers Initiative to teach over 100 secondary school students photography. She’s also a volunteer at Conversation Café.

She’s the convener of THE SHUTTER PATH; an impact outreach for still and motion creative’s which is focused on educating creative’s on the best business practices to enable them grow sustainable businesses and build reputable brands.
